Common Questions

GOT
QUESTIONS?

Can complete beginners join Epic Fitness? +
Absolutely. We specialize in taking men from zero to elite. Your first session includes a full assessment and program build with a certified coach. No experience required — only the willingness to show up and work.
Will I get a custom training and nutrition plan? +
Yes. On Alpha and Elite plans, every member gets a personalized training program and nutrition framework built around their specific body type, goals, and schedule. Cookie-cutter programs are for cookie-cutter gyms.
How fast will I start seeing results? +
Most men notice visible strength gains within 2–3 weeks. Significant physique changes typically appear within 6–8 weeks when following the program consistently. Our top transformers see dramatic results in 90 days.
Is personal training included in my membership? +
Warrior plan members have access to group classes. Alpha members receive 4 PT sessions per month. Elite members get unlimited personal training with a dedicated 1-on-1 coach. All plans include a free onboarding session.
Is this really men-only? Why? +
Yes — men-only, always. This environment is specifically designed to eliminate distractions and social hesitation so men train at maximum intensity. No judgment. No performance anxiety. Just brotherhood and results.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. All memberships are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. We don't trap you — we earn your loyalty through results. Cancel any time with 30 days notice. No penalties, no drama.
